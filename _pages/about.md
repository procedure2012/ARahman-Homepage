## About Me

I am a third-year PhD candidate at the **UC Berkeley Speech & Audio Lab**, where my research focuses on **speech processing and audio machine learning**. My work aims to advance the state of the art in speech technologies, particularly for under-represented languages and low-resource settings.

## Education

- **Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering** (2021–Present) — University of California, Berkeley
- **B.Sc.** (2016–2021) — University of Dhaka

## Research Interests

- Low-Resource Speech Recognition
- Neural Speech Synthesis
- Self-Supervised Audio Representation Learning

## Research Experience

- **Research Intern** — Meta AI Audio (June 2025 – Present)
- **Research Intern** — Amazon Alexa (May 2024 – August 2024)
